# Go-to-Market Strategy

Building the app is only half the battle. Acquiring the "hard-to-reach" blue-collar demographic requires a non-traditional marketing approach.

## 1. The "Watermark" Growth Loop

Tradespeople love sharing their work on Instagram. TradeFolio leverages this behavior rather than competing with it.

### Mechanism
When a user shares a project from TradeFolio to Instagram Stories, the app automatically generates:
- A sleek, branded image overlay
- Project stats (e.g., "500ft Conduit Run - Verified on TradeFolio")
- A QR code linking to the full project

### Result
Every user becomes a billboard, driving their followers to download the app to see full project details.

### Implementation
1. Design professional share templates
2. Auto-generate branded overlays on export
3. Include deep links and QR codes
4. Track referral attribution

## 2. Strategic Partnerships

### 2.1 Trade Schools & Unions

**Target Partners:**
- IBEW (International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers)
- UA (United Association of Plumbers and Pipefitters)
- Local vocational colleges and technical schools
- Apprenticeship programs

**Offer:**
Provide the software free to students as their "Digital Portfolio" for graduation.

**Value:**
- Captures users at the beginning of their career
- Maximizes Lifetime Value (LTV)
- Creates institutional endorsement
- Builds habit early in career

### 2.2 Tool Brands

**Target Partners:**
- Milwaukee Tool
- DeWalt
- Makita
- Klein Tools
- Snap-on

**Campaign Example:**
"Show us your best DeWalt setup on TradeFolio to win a drill kit."

**Value:**
- Brands desperate for user-generated content (UGC)
- Access to hard-to-reach demographic
- Authentic product placement
- Contest drives app downloads

### 2.3 Industry Events

**Opportunities:**
- World of Concrete
- CONEXPO-CON/AGG
- AHR Expo (HVAC)
- FABTECH (Welding)

**Tactics:**
- Demo booths with instant portfolio creation
- "Get your badge" verification stations
- Contest entries for attendees

## 3. Localized SEO Domination

### Strategy
Generate public web pages for every user profile and project:
- `tradefolio.com/pro/john-smith-electrician-phoenix`
- `tradefolio.com/projects/kitchen-remodel-phoenix-az`

### Benefits
These pages index highly for "long-tail" keywords:
- "LVP flooring installer Phoenix"
- "Commercial electrician near me"
- "Licensed plumber Scottsdale reviews"

### Result
When a homeowner searches Google, they find TradeFolio profiles, driving:
- Free organic traffic
- Client leads to users (value prop)
- App downloads from discovery

### Technical Implementation
1. Server-side rendering for SEO
2. Schema.org markup for rich snippets
3. Location-based URL structure
4. Project galleries with alt text

## 5. Referral Program

### Recruiter Referrals

## 7. Launch Sequence

### Pre-Launch (4 weeks before)
1. Build waiting list landing page
2. Partner outreach to trade schools
3. Influencer seeding (trade YouTubers)
4. Press kit preparation

### Launch Week
1. App store release
2. Press release to trade publications
3. Partner announcements
4. Social media campaign launch
5. Referral program activation

### Post-Launch (Ongoing)
1. Weekly feature releases
2. User success story spotlights
3. Partnership expansions
4. Community building events
